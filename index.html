How to Make Pottery

Pottery is the art of shaping clay into functional or decorative objects through hand-building or wheel-throwing, then glazing and firing them in a kiln. Here‚Äôs how to start your clay journey!

Materials & Tools

  • Clay (earthenware, stoneware, or porcelain)
  • Pottery wheel (for wheel-throwing)
  • Hand tools (ribs, wire cutter, trimming tools)
  • Glazes and brushes
  • A kiln for firing

Techniques

  • Hand-building: pinch, coil, and slab methods
  • Wheel-throwing: centering clay, pulling walls, shaping forms

Glazing & Firing

After your clay piece dries, it‚Äôs bisque-fired, glazed for color, and fired again for a glossy finish. Always follow studio safety rules and kiln instructions.

Getting Started Checklist

  • Take an intro class

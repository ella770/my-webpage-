How To Make Pottery

Pottery is when you make cool things out of clay and then bake them in a kiln! Here are some things you need and how to do it.

Materials & Tools

  • Clay (you can buy it at an art store)
  • Pottery wheel (optional)
  • Glazes and brushes
  • A kiln (or use one at a studio)

Basic Steps

  • 1. Shape your clay with your hands or a wheel.
  • 2. Let it dry a bit.

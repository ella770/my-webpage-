How To Make Pottery

Pottery is when you shape clay into something like a bowl, cup, or vase and then fire it in a kiln. Here are some basics!

What You Need

  • Clay (buy it at an art store)
  • Water and some tools
  • A Spinning Wheel
  • A kiln (or a studio that has one)
  • Glaze if you want to make it colorful

Basic Steps

  • 1. Shape your clay with your hands or a wheel.
  • 2. Let it dry a bit
  • 3. Put it in the kiln to harden it.
  • 4. Paint it with glaze and fire/put it in the kiln again.

How To Make Pottery

  • 1. Make sure your hands are wet throught the whole prosses especially if clay starts to feel rough.
  • 2. Center the clay and make sure your arms are locked so you can use your mucles to move the clay.
  • 3. press down in the middle to open the clay, then flatten the bottom a little.
  • 4. put one hand inside the clay (to support it) and the other outside (to shape it). Your fingers should be right across from each other, like they’re gently pinching the clay.
  • 5. while the wheel spins press your fingers gently together to pinch the clay a little.
  • 6. Slowly move your hands upward together, this makes the wall taller and thinner.
  • (Keep the pressure light and even. Go slow and steady)
